PROJECT 01

Brew Haven — Hyper-Local Performance Funnel

Status: Concept Project

This is a self-directed learning project built to practice campaign thinking and measurement planning — not a live client engagement. The scenario is realistic; the business is fictional.

Scenario

A neighborhood café sees strong weekend footfall but empty seats on weekdays, while nearby remote workers looking for a workspace go untapped.

Proposed Approach

A hyper-local landing page and ₹499 weekday workspace pass, paired with a measurement plan I designed to practice mapping conversion events to GTM and GA4 as I learn the tools.
