PROJECT 01

Brew Haven — Hyper-Local Performance Funnel

Status: Concept Project

This is a self-directed learning project built to practice campaign thinking, paid media mapping, and measurement planning — not a live client engagement. The scenario is realistic; the business is fictional.

Scenario

A neighborhood café sees strong weekend footfall but empty seats on weekdays, while nearby remote workers looking for a workspace go untapped.

Proposed Approach

A hyper-local landing page and ₹499 weekday workspace pass, paired with an intent-driven Google Ads matrix and a measurement architecture tracking custom events to GTM and GA4.

View Demo Prototype →
